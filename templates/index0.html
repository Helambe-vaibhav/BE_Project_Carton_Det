Necessity of Carton Detection

Carton detection is essential in warehouse management and logistics. It helps in identifying and tracking cartons containing goods, optimizing inventory management, and ensuring efficient distribution processes.

Advantages of Carton Detection

Using carton detection technology, warehouses can automate tasks such as inventory counting, picking, and sorting. It enables real-time monitoring of stock level
